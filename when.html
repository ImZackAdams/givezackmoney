When Did All This Happen?

Time flies, especially when you're trying to understand the mysteries of web design and coding. Here's a quick (and slightly exaggerated) rundown of Zack's illustrious journey:

  • January 2022
    Zack discovers the 'Inspect Element' tool and mistakenly believes he's hacked the internet.
  • March 2022
    Creates his first website, which consisted of a neon green background and a looping GIF of a dancing banana. Critics called it "an assault on the eyes."
  • June 2022
    Zack reads an article on web design trends and subsequently declares blinking text and marquees the future of design.
  • September 2022
    Launches "GiveZackMoney.com" in hopes of funding his journey to spread the gospel.
  • The Future...
    Who knows? With your support, maybe Zack will finally understand the difference between classes and IDs in CSS. Fingers crossed!
