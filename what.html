What Will Zack Do (and NOT Do) With Your Money?

Your hard-earned cash won't just disappear into the void! Zack has some... interesting plans for every cent received. Here's a breakdown:

What Zack WILL Do:

  • Web Development Courses:
    To improve Zack's "unique" design skills and maybe learn what responsive design actually means.
  • A New Rubber Duck:
    Zack's current rubber duck has heard too much. A fresh listener is needed for those intense debugging sessions.
  • More Coffee:
    Because coding runs on coffee, and Zack needs all the caffeine he can get.
  • A Fancy New Chair:
    For better lumbar support. Zack's current chair squeaks and wobbles, which he claims "adds character."
  • Random Gadgets and Toys:
    Because who doesn't need a desk toy that distracts them from actual work?

What Zack WON'T Do:

  • Buy an Island:
    Because, let's be real, managing an island seems like a lot of work.
  • Invest in Squirrel Startups:
    Even if they promise the next big thing in acorn technology.
  • Fund a Time Machine Project:
    Zack's pretty sure messing with the space-time continuum is a bad idea.
  • Purchase a Pet Dinosaur:
    Despite how cool it might sound, Zack isn't ready for that level of commitment.
  • Build a Lava Lamp Fortress:
    Safety first! And Zack's not sure about the structural integrity of lava lamps.
