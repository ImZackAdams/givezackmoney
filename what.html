What's This All About?

At first glance, this website might seem like a quirky joke. And, well, it partly is. But there's more to it than just neon colors and Zack's questionable design skills.

The "GiveZackMoney" initiative was born out of Zack's genuine desire to improve. He realized that in the vast world of web development, there's always something new to learn. Whether it's mastering the nuances of CSS, understanding the intricacies of JavaScript, or just getting a grip on good design principles (which he clearly needs).

So, while every chuckle, eye-roll, or facepalm this site induces is a win for Zack, there's also a genuine hope behind it. Hope that maybe, with a little help (and money), Zack can elevate his skills from "laughably bad" to just "mediocre". Baby steps, right?

And who knows? Your donation might just fund Zack's next online course, design book, or (let's be real) a much-needed coffee for those late-night coding sessions. Every bit helps!
