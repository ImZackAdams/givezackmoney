A Day in the Life of Zack

Ever wondered what our self-proclaimed quirky, comical, mad genius does during his day? Let's take a peek into the hilariously peculiar world of Zack!

  • 3:00 AM: Wakes up after dreaming of a unicorn-led orchestra. Jots down idea for a "Unicorn-Horn Flute".
  • 4:15 AM: Attempts to make coffee using a contraption that involves marbles, dominoes, and a rubber duck. Ends up ordering a latte from a 24/7 cafe instead.
  • 5:30 AM: Yoga session with his pet rock, Rocky. Rocky excels at the "stillness" pose.
  • 7:00 AM: Breakfast: A smoothie made of bananas, chocolate, and a hint of mathematical equations.
  • 9:00 AM: Tinkers in his workshop. Today's project: shoes that squeak in Morse code.
  • 12:00 PM: Lunch with his talking plant, Fern. Fern just photosynthesizes.
  • 3:00 PM: Takes a "power nap". Dreams of a world where socks rule and humans are worn on feet. Wakes up grateful.
  • 5:00 PM: Tea time with his imaginary friend, Sir Fluffington. Discusses the socioeconomic implications of teddy bear inflation.
  • 8:00 PM: Dinner: A plate of spaghetti tangled in the shape of his favorite constellations.
  • 10:00 PM: Writes in his journal. Today's entry: "Why do zippers never get stuck in dreams? Must investigate."
  • 12:00 AM: Bedtime. Reads a bedtime story to his collection of mismatched buttons. Tonight's pick: "The Button Who Dreamed of Being a Coin".
