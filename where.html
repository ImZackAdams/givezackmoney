Where Does Zack Work?

Zack's inspiration and productivity are heavily influenced by his surroundings. From conventional spots to out-of-the-box locations, here's where Zack gets down to business:

  • The Home Office:
    Equipped with a desk, a half-working laptop, and a chair that squeaks every time Zack leans back. It's basic, but it's home.
  • Local Coffee Shops:
    Where Zack pretends to work but is usually just people-watching or trying out the newest coffee blend.
  • Park Benches:
    Fresh air, chirping birds, and the occasional squirrel stealing Zack's snacks. Nature's inspiration at its finest.
  • The Bathroom:
    You'd be surprised how many of Zack's "bright" ideas originate here. It's a space of solitude and... contemplation.
